What am I looking at?  LATE = leverage-weighted impact per 100 possessions. RAPM = overall adjusted plus-minus. Elevation = how much a player raises teammates' shooting efficiency (pts per 100 shot attempts) when on court. The web shows who a player makes better (amber) and who makes them better (teal). Headshots via cdn.nba.com.
Honest caveat: elevation is unadjusted on/off (WOWY) — role players who only share the floor with stars can creep up; treat it as a lens, not gospel.
